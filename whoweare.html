--- title: Who Are We? description: A little more about Jerry and Caroline Jones ---

Jerry and Caroline Jones have been running small group tours to France since 1999.

Jerry, a Christian minister for over 40 years, was educated at USC and Yale, with further  postgraduate studies at Oxford University, England. They met and married in Oxford in 1978. Caroline graduated from the University of Birmingham, UK, with a Bachelor of Law degree.

After raising their three children in Oxford, the Jones family moved to California, where Caroline became a teacher of French at a  local  public high school.

The Joneses have had a home in France since 1992. They are well-known and respected in the local area.

Passionate about educational travel, Jerry and Caroline have established Summer School France to bring European History, art, architecture and culture to YOU.
