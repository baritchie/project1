Why not think of joining a small group of high schoolers for a 10 night visit to France. Three glorious days in Paris, and 7 in the idyllic countryside of lower Normandy. Visit the Loire Valley, D Day beaches, loads of châteaux (medieval and Renaissance) , Roman ruins, Renaissance art and architecture, museums, school, farm, shopping, and on and on.The 7 days in the countryside will be in an 18th century mansion.

The cost is $1950 PLUS airfare, and this covers about everything: all accommodation (including three nights in Paris), all transportation within France; all meals (except Paris…to allow choice), all entrance fees into sites; and travel insurance.
